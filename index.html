Pascuala Galay Martínez

The best mother, wife, and daughter in the whole world

Pascuala Galay Martínez

(May 17, 1960 - Nov 12, 2018)

Loved daughter, unmatched mother, incredible sister, devote wife, awesome chruch women's leader, and there are a thousand things more to say about her. For me is easy to describe her with just one word:

Perfect

She lived to give her children the life she couldn't have, worked tirelessly for years, even when wasn't necessary, from her I learned, not only write or read, but work for that I really want, and never give up even against the whole world.


With her, I learn how to keep myself on my way, with or without strength. I'm who I am because of her. And there's nothing I would change from her, 'cause, of course, she wasn't perfect, but without our mistakes, we aren't ourselves. That she taught me.


There are a lot of things that I want to tell you, but, you always know what's in my head even before I say anything, but... I love you and miss you like I never mind.


After all, I thank God. I still remember all those times when she used to say:

My prayer is that God let me raise my children, and, someday meet my grandchildren


I know that I'm going to see you again, so, thanks, thanks for all this you gave to me. I know I don't deserve this, but anyway, you mom, you are awesome.
